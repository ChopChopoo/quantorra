Frequently asked questions

Common questions about Quantorra shoes and ordering.

Is the Range really suitable for both easy and tempo runs?

Yes for runners who train at recreational paces. Competitive runners doing track sessions will likely want a dedicated speed trainer; for everyone else the Range covers easy through tempo comfortably.

How does the BalancedFlex midsole differ from a single-density foam?

BalancedFlex layers a slightly firmer carrier foam under a softer top layer. The top layer absorbs initial impact, the carrier provides response on toe-off.

What is the buyback program?

Send any worn Quantorra back to us and we will recycle the materials responsibly. Buyback credit is $20 per pair toward your next order.

How long should the Range last?

We design the Range for 400 to 500 miles. The dual-density heel is the limiting factor; the upper typically outlasts the midsole.

What is the difference between the Range and the Range LR?

The LR has 2 mm more midsole. Same upper, same outsole. Choose the LR if your typical long run is 12 miles or more.

Where do you ship?

We ship within the US and Canada. International shipping is available through our Toronto warehouse for a flat $20 fee.
