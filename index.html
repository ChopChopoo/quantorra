Quantorra

One shoe, every run.

Quantorra was started in 2020 with a single-shoe philosophy: most runners do not need three pairs in rotation, they need one good pair that does most things well. The BalancedFlex midsole is tuned for the broad middle of training - easy runs, steady efforts, the occasional tempo session - without specialising in any of them. The brand sells direct from a single warehouse in Salt Lake City, and operates a buyback program that takes worn shoes back for responsible recycling.
