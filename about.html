About Quantorra

Quantorra was founded by Anita Reyes and Joel Wakefield, who met in 2019 at a sports-engineering conference in Munich. Anita, a former competitive cross-country runner, had spent six years at a major athletic-shoe company on the running-product team. Joel had run a small biomechanics consulting practice for a decade and worked with podiatrists across the western US. Both had reached the same conclusion independently: the running-shoe industry was building too many specialised shoes for runners who didn't need them, and not enough good general-purpose shoes for runners who did. They started Quantorra in 2020 in a Salt Lake City warehouse, with a mission statement that fits on one line: build the one pair a recreational runner needs. The first BalancedFlex prototype shipped in October 2021 and the production model launched the following spring.

Quantorra is based in Salt Lake City, Utah and ships direct.
